What is Pollution?

Pollution is the addition of any substance or form of energy to the environment at a rate faster than it can be dispersed or stored in a harmless form. It is mostly caused by the burning of fossil fuels, such as coal, oil, natural gas and gasoline (that is used to produce electricity and power vehicles).
