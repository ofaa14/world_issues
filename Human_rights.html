Lack of Access to Clean Water

What is it?

The state of not physically being able to have safe and readily available water. Clean water is important for public health, whether it is used for drinking, domestic use, food production or recreational purposes. There are over 844 million people globally that are affected by this issue.

Violence Against Women

What is it?

A global pandemic that comes in many forms. The forms of violence include; physical, sexual, and psychological (both in public and private spaces). One out of three women deal with the troubles of being physically or sexually abused by an intimate partner.

LGBTQ+ Discrimination

What is it?

A predisposition towards heterosexual people, which is biased against lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, and queer people, among others. Many LGBTQ+ individuals often feel alone due to the outrageous and unrighteous acts which discriminate against them. {LGBTQ+ Stattistics: 85% have experienced verbal harassment; 58% have felt unsafe at school due to their sexual orientation; 43% have felt unsafe because of their gender identity; 27% have been physically harassed at school because of their sexual orientation; 13% have been physically harassed because of their gender identity.}
